About

Who we are

We're a small student robotics team based in Bangladesh. We build autonomous hardware using locally available parts, document the whole process, and publish everything open-source.

The goal isn't to impress it's to build real things, learn from them, and make it easy for others to do the same.

BdSL Translator Glove

Wearable glove that translates Bangladeshi Sign Language into speech in real-time. Flex sensors are 3D printed from conductive TPU no commercial sensors needed. XIAO ESP32-C3 + MPU-6050, custom KiCad PCB (0 DRC errors), Fusion 360 dual-material exoskeleton. Also building an open-source BdSL gesture dataset. PCB fabrication pending.
