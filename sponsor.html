Support Team Bumblebee

We're a small student team from Bangladesh building open-source autonomous hardware. Any support that helps us keep building is genuinely appreciated, big or small.

What we need

We're not asking for cash. The most useful support is manufacturing and components that directly unblock our builds.

Our current projects: a modular autonomous rover (open-source chassis, ~3kg printed parts, custom PCBA) and a Bangladeshi Sign Language translator glove (conductive TPU flex sensors, ESP32, custom PCB).

3D Printing

The rover chassis has ~3kg of printed parts. FDM in PLA or PETG. This is our biggest bottleneck right now.

PCB Assembly (PCBA)

Custom motor control and power distribution boards. KiCad files ready, just need fabrication and assembly.

Components

Motors, servos, sensors, bearings, fasteners. Anything off the BOM that's hard to source locally in Bangladesh.

Mentorship

Engineers or makers willing to review our designs, give feedback, or connect us with resources.

What you get

✓

Logo on every project page

Your name and logo on the rover page, glove page, and any future project that uses your support.

✓

Credit in all documentation and build logs

Every build log entry and README that mentions supported hardware will credit you by name.

✓

Exposure across future builds

The rover chassis is a platform, not a single project. Sponsors of the chassis get visibility on every application built on top of it.

✓

Community post (for PCBWay sponsors)

We'll publish a detailed project post on the PCBWay community platform crediting your support.

✓

Honest documentation of your product

We'll document how we used your parts or service in the build. Real usage, not a paid promotion.
