Why sponsor
Engineering value, not just goodwill

Your support isn't a donation into a void, it produces documented, replicable engineering that the open-source hardware community can build on.

Everything is open-source

Every schematic, KiCad file, STEP file, firmware, and build log is published on GitHub. Sponsor credit lives permanently in that documentation.

Real-world robotics experimentation

Bumblebee is being developed as a modular autonomous rover platform intended for outdoor robotics and autonomous navigation projects and competitions.

STEM impact in an underserved region

We're building in Bangladesh with locally available parts, proving open hardware is replicable anywhere. Sponsors enable that model to exist.

Honest product documentation

If you sponsor components or fabrication, we document exactly how we used them. Real performance data. No paid-promotion language.

Active prototype
Current needs

We're in active prototype phase. These are the specific items blocking progress right now.

Modular Autonomous Rover — Prototype 1

In progress
1

3D Printed Structure Critical

~3 kg of PLA/PETG parts, chassis frame, motor mounts, steering brackets, wheel hubs. Designed in Fusion 360, STEP files on GitHub.

2

Custom PCBA Critical

Motor control and power distribution boards. Needs fabrication and SMD assembly.

3

JGB37-520 DC Gear Motors Needed

6× gear motors for drivetrain. Hard to source reliably in Bangladesh at consistent spec.

4

LDX-227 Servos Needed

Steering and payload mechanism actuation. 4× required per chassis configuration.

5

Power System & Compute Planned

LiPo battery pack, BMS, and compute board (Raspberry Pi or equivalent) for autonomous navigation stack.

What your support funds

Every category maps directly to a subsystem of the rover. Support can target a specific area or go toward the overall build.

Category Subsystem Key components
Drivetrain
 Locomotion JGB37-520 DC gear motors, wheels, motor drivers BTS7960
Motion
 Steering & payload LDX-227 servos, linkage hardware, servo horns
Electronics
 Control & sensing XIAO ESP32-C3, MPU-6050, GPS modules, Raspberry pi
Manufacturing
 Structural prototype FDM 3D printing (PLA/PETG), PCB fabrication & assembly
Power
 Energy management LiPo battery pack, BMS, power distribution, regulators
Compute
 Autonomy stack SBC (Raspberry Pi / Jetson Nano), ROS 2, SLAM, GPS nav
